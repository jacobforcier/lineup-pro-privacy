Privacy Policy

Lineup Pro  ·  Last updated: March 16, 2026

Lineup Pro is a youth baseball coaching tool for managing rosters, game lineups, pitch counts, and player statistics. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is stored.

Information We Collect

Lineup Pro does not collect any personal information. All data you enter — including team names, player names, jersey numbers, game schedules, pitch counts, and statistics — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage (UserDefaults). No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Data Storage

All app data resides on your device and is subject to Apple's standard iOS backup and security policies. If you back up your device to iCloud or iTunes/Finder, app data may be included in that backup and is protected by your Apple account security settings. We have no access to any backup data.

Third-Party Services

Lineup Pro does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. No SDKs that collect user data are included in the app.

Children's Privacy

Lineup Pro is designed for use by coaches and parents managing youth sports teams. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about children. Player entries within the app contain only first names and jersey numbers at the coach's discretion, and that information never leaves the device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any information with third parties. The Share Lineup Card feature generates an image from your on-device data and shares it using iOS's native share sheet — the destination is chosen entirely by you and the data is not routed through our systems.

Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete all app data by removing the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
